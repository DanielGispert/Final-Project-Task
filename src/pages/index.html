A popurri is a mixture or combination of different elements, themes, or styles in a single presentation or composition. The term is commonly used in music, where it refers to a compilation of fragments or parts of various songs that are performed in a continuous sequence. In a musical popurri, fragments of different songs are usually combined into a single performance. In addition to music, the term "popurri" is also used in other contexts. For example, in gastronomy, a popurry may be a mixture of different ingredients, flavors, or textures on a dish. In the literary field, a popurri may refer to a collection of different writings, poems or stories in a single book. In short, a popurri is a combination or mixture of different elements, whether in music, gastronomy, literature or other fields, that are presented together in a joint or continuous manner.

About Can Joan:

Can Joan is a family restaurant located in a charming costero pueblo. As a reputación bien stablecida, it has become a popular destination both for los lugareosos and for los touristes who seek to be delighted as an authentic gastronomy experience.

The setting in Can Joan is acogedor y cálido, with a rustic decoration that refles the esencia del ambiento costero. Las mesas is so that every commensal can enjoy from sight at sea mientras gustea exquisitos platos.

Can Joan's menu is based on the traditional local cocina, with notable local flavors and fresh ingredients. The dishes are elaborated with pasión y cuidado, combining recetas caseras transmitidas de generación en generación con toques creativos y modernos.

About Tinder Dog:

TinderDog's idea would be to provide a virtual spacio donde los amantes de los perros puedan contrarto otros individuos comparing su amor y pasión por los perros. The platform would allow users to search and connect with people who tie perros or who are interested in interacting with ellos.

Like Tinder, the user could create profiles, including information about the mismos and sus perros. They could add photos of their pets, share details such as raza, edad and personality of sus perros, and explain their own interests and preferential relationdos con los perros.

TinderDog's main idea would be to facilitate the interaction and connection between persons with a common interest in perros. Users could delizar hacia la derecha or hacia la izquierda in the profiles of other persons, expresando su interés or desinterés. If two people are going to have mutual interest, they can begin to communicate through the platform and, potentially, organize encuentros para que sus perros se conozcan and enjoy activists together.
